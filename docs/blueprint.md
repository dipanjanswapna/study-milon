# **App Name**: FocusFlow

## Core Features:

- Personalized Home Dashboard: A customizable home page displaying current study timers, quick links to notes, and progress at a glance.
- Study Timer & Breaks: Implement a flexible timer for study sessions (e.g., Pomodoro) with integrated break notifications and progress visualization.
- Note & Resource Repository: Allow users to create, save, and categorize text notes or link external study resources for easy access.
- Simple Goal Tracking: Enable users to set and track daily or weekly study goals, with visual feedback on their achievement.
- AI Study Prompt Generator: An AI tool that generates personalized study questions or prompts based on keywords, topics, or notes provided by the user.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional purplish-blue (#4D40B2) to evoke focus and intellectual depth, providing a solid foundation against the light background.
- Background color: A very subtle, cool-toned off-white (#ECEEF4) with a hint of the primary hue, ensuring maximum readability and a clean aesthetic for long study sessions.
- Accent color: A vibrant and clear sky blue (#42B3F4) used sparingly for interactive elements, progress indicators, and call-to-action buttons to draw attention without being overwhelming.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for its readability, modern look, and touch of warmth, suitable for extensive reading and clear presentation of information.
- Utilize a consistent set of minimal, outline-style icons to maintain a clean and uncluttered interface, reinforcing clarity and functionality.
- A structured, modular layout with clear separation of sections to guide user attention and present information logically, facilitating a focused user experience.
- Subtle, fluid transitions and micro-interactions for timer progress, button feedback, and content loading, enhancing perceived responsiveness and user engagement without distraction.
